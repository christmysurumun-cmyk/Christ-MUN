About Christ-MUN

Welcome, Future Diplomats!

Christ Public School is proud to host its annual Model United Nations conference. MUN is an academic simulation where students step into the shoes of ambassadors from UN member states to debate current global issues.

Our mission at Christ-MUN is to provide a platform for young leaders to develop their skills in public speaking, diplomacy, research, and critical thinking. Join us for a weekend of inspiring debate and collaborative problem-solving.
